Buffalo Wings

The best wings for any American occasion

Theres always a day for wings. The Super Bowl, 4th of July, or any holiday. Wings are America's favorite food!

Ingredients

  • Deep frying oil
  • 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 slinless, boneless chicken brests, cut into 1/2 inch strips
  • 1/4 cup hot pepper sauce
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine flour, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, paprika, and garlic powder in a large bowl. Whisk together egg and milk in a small bowl.
  3. Dip each piece of chicken in egg mixture, then roll in flour mixture. Repeat so that each piece of chicken is double-coated. Refrigerate breaded chicken for 20 minutes.
  4. Fry chicken in hot oil in batches. Cook until golden brown and juices run clear, 5 to 6 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  5. Combine hot sauce and butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat sauce in the microwave until melted, 20 to 30 seconds. Pour sauce over fried chicken; mix to coat.
